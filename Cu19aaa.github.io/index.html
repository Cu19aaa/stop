About

My name is David

I am a recent university graduate. I studied computer science with a focus is software engineering between 2019 and 2022 at the university of Hertfordshire and at the end of my program i came out with a 2.1. Upon finishing university and being faced with the job market i realised that due to the circumstance of the time during which i was at university i had failed to acquire any proper marketable job experience seeing as for the most of my university period i was in lockdown. On finishing i decided that web development and frontend development was the path i wanted to follow and as such i needed a plan to stick to. At the start of 2023 i signed up to a coding bootcamp which was from freeCodeCamp Academy which was what taught me the basics of web development and how to begin. After completing the bootcamp i went on to try challenges from the website frontend mentor, and most of the challenges i tried out are what are displayed on my portfolio. My HTML and CSS skills are at a comfortible 65% maybe 70% but im still learning everyday. My JavaScript is where i struggle a little bit sitting at a 10% however i am still in the early days of my career and im still growing and learning.Most of the work ive been doing are learning how to make landing pages and learning how to use css objects to properly style html pages. Ive also been learning how to make any website i make respond to the size of the screen it is viewed on(Device responsive websites)
